Stages

This roadmap outlines our OCEAN stages and where we want to take the Fuzion NFT Universe. A lot of new concepts will be introduced step by step. The evolution and revolution of Fuzion NFTs are nearing. Join us as we journey into new and beautiful worlds with some of the most original art ever seen in the crypto space!

01 Originals 02 CHAINED 03 EVOLUTION 04 ADVENTURE 05 NIRVANA

Stage 01

Originals

Quality over quantity. Fuzion NFT stage 1 Originals will be NFTs that will stand out from the rest. Our goal was to handcraft original NFTs that are unique from all other NFTs out there. These originals are created with great thought, precision and intricacy. Fuzion Originals will establish the gold standard with which we’ll move forward in our NFT line-up. Fuzion token holders (Rice, TeslaSafe, and Paz) will have first access privileges when it comes to the minting phase of Fuzion Original NFTs.

Stage 02

CHAINED

All Fuzion NFTs can be used as digital avatars in the Fuzion Universe (Metaverse). Unlike many other NFTs out there, Stage 2 of Fuzion NFTs aka Chained will have both the metadata and art of Fuzion Anime Punks stored on the blockchain. Fuzion Chained solves many problems with the existing IPFS storage protocol that most NFTs use. Fuzion development team is always thinking ahead about the safety and security of its community. For many NFTs, if the IPFS systems were to shut down, hundreds of millions of dollars worth of assets could equally get wiped out in a matter of seconds. Stage 2 Chained aims to solve this predicament. Even if IPFS systems were to fail or shut down, all Fuzion NFTs would still be made available to all their owners because it will be stored on the actual blockchain.

Just like Originals, no Fuzion Chained NFTs can be identical. If you try to create a Fuzion Chained Anime Punk that has the exact same traits as Anime Punk Originals, the contract will deny it. Stage 2 of the Fuzion NFTs is a revolutionary approach to making sure your ownership to your NFTs are yours forever on the blockchain.

Stage 03

EVOLUTION

The tragedy in most crypto projects is not in failure, but rather in complacency; not in our doing too much, but rather in our doing too little. The Fuzion Team is not out to prove anything, but sets out to improve in everything. Our goal is to continually evolve into something of greater value and use. That’s where Stage 3, Fuzion Evolution comes into play.

Gearing up for the Fuzion Universe (Metaverse) the Evolution Stage seeks to expand into the 3D world. Since the Fuzion Universe will exist in a 3 dimensional space, the Fuzion Anime Punks will also progress into a 3D avatars. Furthermore, owners of the Fuzion Stage 01 Originals will have direct access in the presale minting process to mint their 3D equivalent. In Stage 01 Originals, we’re introduced to 3/4 body type NFTs. In Stage 02, we’re introduced to full body NFTs and with Stage 03 Evolution, the NFTs fully mature into full body 3D Anime Punks that also have the ability to move and become animated.

In Evolution, we’ll see more options to build on top of your avatar to truly represent who you are. Additional creative options and attributes will be available to add to the head, face, mouth, arms, hands, body, legs and feet. Players will also be able to add specific movements and poses to create a more exceptional experience.

Stage 04

ADVENTURE

The Adventure Phase will see the development, unveiling and exploration into the much anticipated Fuzion Universe. Here we will have a sneak peek of what the Fuzion Universe will consist of. You’ll now be able to take your Fuzion Anime Punk and insert it into a whole new world of possibilities and endless adventure. The ability to play and earn begins here and will continue to expand out into Stage 05 - Nirvana.

The Fuzion Universe will consist of land, water, air and space. There will be a Fuzion World, much like the real world where you’ll be able to own land, commercial, industrial and residential zoning. You’ll be able to decorate your home with art pieces and other valuable items. You’ll be able to own pets, you’ll be able to drive cars, aeroplanes, hovercrafts, boats and various other vehicles. You’ll also be introduced to specific items that enhance your character. Various items can also have decentralized finance applications. For example, you may be able to own NFTs that increase the value of your farming APR or APY. There will be NFTs that are used just for staking.

The adventure phase will introduce monsters in the world that you can defeat and destroy for treasures. The more difficult the level of monsters, the higher level of loot you’ll receive. There are wearable NFTs that enhance your player’s ability to farm in-game loot or increase the chances of getting higher level loot.
