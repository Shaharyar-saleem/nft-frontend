Why Fuzion

Whilst minting, Fuzion Punk utilized a provably fair system with randomization; this meant that everyone who minted one of the original 10,000 had an equal chance of minting a rare Fuzion Punk. If you are reading this now and missed the original mint, don't lose hope. Join the discord, trade, barter and enter competitions to get your very own.

Minting Fees

Minting Fee: The cost of mining a unique Fuzion Punk NFT

  • 14% to NFT Holders
  • 10% to referrals (if no referral exists then will be split between 5 & 6)
  • 4% to PAZ holders
  • 20% to Team Expenses
  • 20% to Team Expenses
  • 30% to Development Fund DAO

Royalties

Royalties: Fees from the Fuzion NFT Marketplace.

The Listing and Delisting Fee is to prevent users from spamming the marketplace.

  • 7% reflections to all NFT Holders
  • 70% royalties to the original minter
  • 0.05 BNB Listing Fee reflected to all NFT Holders
  • 0.05 BNB Delisting Fee reflected to all NFT Holders
